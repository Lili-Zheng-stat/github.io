Research

Lili Zheng

 

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Rice University, advised by Prof. Genevera Allen.

Previously, I completed my Ph.D. in the Department of Statistics at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, under the supervision of Prof. Garvesh Raskutti. I received my bachelor's degree at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2016.

I am broadly interested in statistical and optimization problems for large-scale, high-dimensional, and dependent data. In particular, I would love to work and collaborate on the following topics: network analysis, graphical model, high-dimensional inference, model-agnostic variable importance, missing data, tensor data analysis, stochastic algorithms, non-convex optimization.
